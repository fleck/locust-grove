# Wall Framing

- **Load Bearing Headers:** Shall be LVL as noted in the window schedule and framing drawings.
- **Non-Load Bearing Headers:** Shall be 2x6 lumber.
- **Dimensional Lumber:** Shall be No. 2 or better.
- **Pressure Treated Lumber:** Shall be used on all lumber in contact with concrete, in addition to a non-permeable foam membrane.
- **Sheathing:** Shall be 7/16" OSB installed vertically with a 1/8" expansion gap between sheets.
